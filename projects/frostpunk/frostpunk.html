Frostpunk is a survival city builder where you take the place of the ruler of the last city on Earth. It is your duty to lead the city by enacting laws and making decisions as well as planning the city's infrastructure. What will you do to keep the generator, the hearth of your city, running? But bevare, if the discontent gets too high, or the hope stoops too low, you may not be long for this world. Learn more about the different scenarios here.

Ruling the city

As the ruler of the city, you need to see to the needs of your citizens. They all need to be feed or they will starve, but the quality of the food can vary. The lower the quality the more discontent and less hope, but at least they will stay alive. But that is not the only thing your citizens needs. Your citizens also need heat and housing, or otherwise they will get sick in the cold, unforgiving world that you find yourslef in. You also need to enact new laws that lets your city adapt to the frozen wasteland, but they all come with their downsides. You are also going to have to make vitals decisions that are going to affect the whole city. But no matter what happens: The city must survive.

Expanding your city

You need to expand you city in order for it to survive. To do this, you not only need to expand the city's infrastructure, but also research new technologies and explore the wasteland for any supplies you can harvest. Researching new technologies will unlock new ways to tackle the frozen winds and are vital for survival. Exploring the wasteland will let you uncover what is left of the old world, and will let you make decisions that can bring in rare resources or lead to the demise of your explorers.
