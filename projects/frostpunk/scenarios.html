Scenarios

There are meny different scenarios that you can play. Each putting you in charge of a different city under different conditions. Each scenario also has a unique story that develops and create different challanges that you have to overcome. All scenarios have a final goeal and can be won, except endless mode, where you instead of a story, just need to survive for as long as possible.

The Last Autum

This Scenario is a prequel to the rest of the game. You are tasked with building a generator at Site 113, thich is meant to house the people of Liverpool. You will be expected to meet various deadlines throughout the scenario. Failing to meet these deadlines repeatedly results in a termination. In this scenario, the themperature starts at +10°C and only decreases slowly, which means that it is nothing to be concerned about until at the very end of the game. Hope is also replaced by motivation. The motivation metre is divided into three thirds giving -30%, +0%, and +30% productivity respectively, and low motivation cannot lead to you execution.

The Fall of Winterhome

In this scenario, you have been put in harge of Winterhome after the previous ruler missmanaged the city leading to a revolt. Now you are tasked with repairing the damages cause by the revolt, and redesigning the city from the horrible mess that your predecessor left behind. Due to the revolt, the starting hope is low and discontent is high. Soon after the immediate problems have been fixed, you will discorver that the generator has been irreperably damaged due to the incompetence of the previous leadership. This means it will have regular breakdowns and will eventually turn off for good. This means you need to spend resources to reapir and build on a nearby Land Dreadnought in order to turn in into a mobile home for the population of Winterhome. But what if it does not fit everyone? Then you have to choose who survives.
